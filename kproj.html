nikola woudstra / archive / about

in this project, pictures were reproduced by hand on light sensitive film for a 35 mm camera. the work was precise, and a lot of information was lost from the original pictures, which resulted in almost abstract images. the process of compressing was countered by additional enlarging: reproducing them once more by hand on big sheets of textile. these works were exhibited in a large installation, posing the question; what remains?
